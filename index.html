SOCIAL LIFE AT ALC

            Every one of us has different and unique personalities. We are all very distinct from being outgoing, reserved, or in between. 
            These unique characteristics have helped us shape our social circles. Who we hang out more with, study with, and carry out our daily activities. 

            For organizing/attending campus events such as IKAZE, through Firecamps and Assemblies to End of Year events, one can find pleasure throughout the year. 
            Additionally, there are opportunities to participate in Chess competitions, laser tag games, and more depending on your creativity. 
            
            Yes, at ALC, we have the opportunity to shape our social environment and the kind of activities we want to carry out. 
            This opportunity we believe has allowed us to explore, move what we like and introduce our passions to others.

EXPERIENCE IN MAURITIUS

            We would like to describe our experience in Mauritius so far as full of discoveries. 
            From visiting the splendid beaches to dining in the awesome restaurants/ fast food shops, 
            the heavenly island hasn’t disappointed us. 

            Yes, Mauritius hasn’t disappointed us. Studying peacefully on campus, carrying out 
            outdoor/indoor activities, finding job opportunities on the island, and more, 
            the choice is ours and realizable.
